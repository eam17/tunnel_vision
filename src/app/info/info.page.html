Help Welcome to Tunnel Vision!

Grid drawing is a method of transfering a reference image onto paper.

Tunnel Vision will overlay an 8x8 grid over your photo. The 8x8 size was chosen to keep each individual square detailed, without having too many squares to draw.

Make an account, log-in and upload the photo you would like to work with. You are able to upload multiple images that will be there when you log in next time!

Click on the uploaded image that you would like to draw. You will see a new page with a grid over your image. Click on the square to zoom into it and click again to zoom back out.

Put an 8x8 grid over your drawing paper and transfer the image square by square untill you have a finished drawing! For best results, work on each square from start to finish instead of trying to work on several squares at a time.
